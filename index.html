Super Square

 

Evade enemies and collect coins in this strategic grid-based game!

 

FOLLOW @KEVEK if you enjoyed this!

 
   

[Instructions]

   
         
  • Click on the arrow buttons to move.
    •      
  • Avoid enemies and going off the grid.
    •      
  • You get a point for every move you make.
    •      
  • Collect coins to earn 3 points.
    •      
  • Red enemies move in a straight line.
    •      
  • Orange enemies move randomly.
    •
